What are my favorite things to do online?

I like to try and find websites people haven't looked at in a long long time! It's like archeology! I get to see a preserved bit of history when I find a website from the 90s that hasn't been updated in 20 years! Sometimes these websites were old chat rooms, personal blogs, forums, or homepages for old businesses. When I find these I get a peek into the lives of these people and what they cared about

I also like to use all those big websites you already know about. I browse Twitter a lot, watch YouTube a lot, sane thing with Hulu, Netflix, and Amazon Prime. I also like to find games that I don't know anything about and try them out without doing any research on them.
